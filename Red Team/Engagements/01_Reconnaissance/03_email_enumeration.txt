==================================================
1. Overview
==================================================
Email enumeration is the process of discovering valid email addresses associated with a target organization.

Email addresses are critical because they:

- serve as usernames for authentication systems
- are targets for phishing campaigns
- enable password spraying attacks
- reveal internal naming conventions

Attackers combine multiple data sources to build accurate email lists.

==================================================
3. Common Email Formats
==================================================

Organizations typically use consistent patterns:

- first.last@example.com
- first@example.com
- f.last@example.com
- firstl@example.com
- lastname@example.com

Identifying the pattern allows attackers to generate large numbers of valid addresses.
